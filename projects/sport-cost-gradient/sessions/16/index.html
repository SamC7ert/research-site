Summary#

Developed and refined interactive React visualization F4b exploring the “U-shape” relationship between equipment costs and inequality’s effect on Olympic success.

  • Boundary test visualizations deferred to separate F4c component.

Principle#

Clean, focused visualizations preferred over complex multi-purpose interfaces.
